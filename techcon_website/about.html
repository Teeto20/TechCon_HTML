History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of developers passionate about innovation. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the leading technology conferences worldwide.

Mission

Our mission is to empower the global tech community through connection, collaboration, and creativity. TechCon is driven by the goal of shaping the future through shared knowledge and visionary thinking.

  • Elon Turing – A visionary in AI development and ethical machine learning.
